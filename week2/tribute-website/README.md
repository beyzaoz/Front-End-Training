## üé® Design Philosophy and CSS Harmony

The design aims to reflect the **melancholic, simple, and profound** literary style of Sabahattin Ali.

* **Color Scheme:** Earth tones, dark gray, and subtle sepia colors are used to evoke the feeling of old paper or a historical photograph. This palette aligns with the atmosphere found in the author's works.
* **Fonts:** Sharp and strong fonts (e.g., Serif) are chosen for headings, while legible and simple fonts (e.g., Sans-Serif) are chosen for the body text.
* **CSS Organization:** Flexbox and Grid structures are used to ensure the page has a user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing appearance on all devices. The biography section, in particular, is structured to improve readability.
* **Visual Harmony:** The main image and citation capture a thoughtful and sorrowful moment, best representing the author's literary legacy and style.
