First-time homebuyers got squeezed last month as prices hit record April high

Rising US home prices are heightening the housing affordability crisis for Americans, especially first-time buyers.The median price of a previously owned home in the US grew 5.7% in April from a year earlier to $407,600, according to data from the National Association of Realtors released Wednesday. That was the fourth consecutive monthly increase and was a record for April prices.
